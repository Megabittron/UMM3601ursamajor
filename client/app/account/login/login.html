Welcome to the URSA Major website!

This is the web tool for the University of Minnesota at Morris' Undergraduate Research Symposium.

From this website, students have the ability to:

  • Submit abstracts for the current academic year's URS.
  • Check in on their submissions during the review and revision process.
  • Modify their submission by "Resubmitting" it.
  • View and make comments on their submissions.
  • Look through past submissions that they have been a part of.

Additionally, this website provides the tools needed for URS committee members to manage submissions.

From this website, committee members have the ability to:

  • Manage the status of submissions as they move through the revision process.
  • Provide comments and feedback on submissions and abstracts.
  • Approve changes made to submissions by students.
  • Filter and sort through submissions they are responsible for.

When first logging in, be sure to go to "Account Information" and enter your t-shirt size for this year's URS.

To get started, all you need to do is log in below with a valid University of Minnesota email address.
